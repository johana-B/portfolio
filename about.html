about me

Hello, I am Yohannes Bekele. I graduated from Arba Minch University with a degree in electrical and computer engineering (computer engineering) and I work as a web developer. I live and work in Ethiopia's capital, Addis Abeba. I spend the most of my day experimenting with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, node JS, ReactJS, and Python (and its endless list of frameworks). I adore coding and the daily challenge of learning something new.

I have experience in web development, particularly with javascript and node js. I've included a few of my projects on my github webpage and in my project page. I am presently employed with Dan Energy Ethiopia as a software engineer.

I am adaptable, versatile, and have outstanding time management abilities. I am a person who is energetic, self-motivated, dependable, responsible, and hardworking. I am a seasoned team player who can adjust to any demanding scenario. I can operate well in a collaborative setting as well as on my own initiative.
